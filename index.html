About Veronica

With over a decade of experience in cinematic photography, Veronica captures the ephemeral beauty of moments with a distinctive eye for composition and light.

Her work has been featured in international exhibitions and private collections, blending fine art aesthetics with documentary authenticity.

Based in Newberry, South Carolina, Veronica runs V Long Studio, offering professional services for weddings, quinceañeras, portraits, and more.

She is known for her passionate dedication to her craft, and her unique ability to make every session personal, expressive, and timeless.
